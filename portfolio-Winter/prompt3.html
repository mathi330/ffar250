For my gif, I decided to use two images of sound waves because the whole idea behind my project is sound in the absence of images or visuals. I thought it would also be interesting to use this prompt to look at how we visualize sound and how people understand what we mean when showing this representation even without the sense of hearing involved, showing the dominance of sight in our society.
